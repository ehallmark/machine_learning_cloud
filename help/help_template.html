Filters

Overview

Filters are how we narrow down a search. Filters can be applied in the Filters tab by clicking the Filters dropdown menu and selecting the desired filter. Multiple filters can also be applied.

Filter Types

There are many different types of filters, mostly based on the type of attribute being filtered on (numeric, date, text, etc.).
